I.Who we are

LEVELED is a fitness tracking app that turns real-world workouts into a roleplaying-game character progression system. This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

  • Operator: Justin, operating LEVELED as an independent developer
  • Location: Frankfurt, Germany
  • Privacy contact: justinjpz33@gmail.com

References to "we," "us," and "our" mean LEVELED. References to "you" mean any user of the LEVELED app.

II.Data we collect

Account data

  • Name or chosen username
  • Email address
  • Hashed password
  • Account creation date

App usage data

  • Class selection (Warrior, Mage, or Beast) and class history
  • Avatar state, equipped gear, owned vehicles
  • Body part XP, levels, and point balances

Device data

  • Push notification token (only if you enable notifications)
  • Anonymized error logs and crash reports

What we do not collect

  • Precise location
  • Photos, camera, or microphone input
  • Contacts
  • Browser history
  • Financial or payment information (the app has no paid features at this time)

IV.WHOOP integration

When you connect WHOOP through the in-app authorization flow:

  • WHOOP shows you exactly which data scopes we request before you authorize. You can decline at any time.
  • We store your WHOOP access and refresh tokens encrypted at rest in our database, linked only to your LEVELED account.
  • We use your WHOOP data to: (a) apply XP multipliers when you log workouts, (b) update your avatar's posture and visual state based on daily recovery, and (c) optionally count WHOOP-tracked workouts toward your cardio vehicle tier progress.
  • You can disconnect WHOOP at any time from Settings → Integrations. Disconnection revokes our access token, stops all webhook delivery, and within 30 days we delete cached WHOOP data tied to your account.
  • We do not share your WHOOP data with any third party.
  • WHOOP's own privacy practices are described at whoop.com/privacy. When you use WHOOP, you remain subject to WHOOP's own privacy policy.

VIII.How we protect your data

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS using TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • Passwords are hashed with bcrypt (or equivalent) — we never store passwords in plain text.
  • WHOOP access and refresh tokens are encrypted at rest using AES-256.
  • Database access is restricted to a minimal set of authenticated services.
  • We log access to user data for audit purposes.
  • In the event of a data breach affecting your personal data, we will notify you and the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, as required by GDPR Article 33.

IX.Your rights

Under GDPR and similar laws, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Delete your account and associated data ("right to erasure")
  • Export your data in a machine-readable format ("right to data portability")
  • Withdraw consent at any time, including disconnecting WHOOP, without affecting prior lawful processing
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Restrict processing in certain circumstances
  • Lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority — for users in Hesse, Germany, that is the Hessischer Beauftragter für Datenschutz und Informationsfreiheit (HBDI)

To exercise any of these rights, email justinjpz33@gmail.com. We respond within 30 days.

You can also:

  • Delete your account directly in Settings → Account → Delete Account
  • Export your workout data in Settings → Integrations & Data → Export Workout Data

X.Children

LEVELED is not intended for users under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 16. If you believe a minor has provided us data, contact justinjpz33@gmail.com and we will delete it.
